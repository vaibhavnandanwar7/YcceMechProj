Metrology & Quality Control (MQC) is the subject concern with study of various measuring instruments and their applications in the industry as well as study of different quality control techniques.

Study of MQC is needed -
  1. To emphasis the need of metrological instrument and its role in quality control.
  2. To enable the students to take up metrology work in industries and using latest metrological equipments.
  3. To impart knowledge of tolerance chart & quality control techniques.
After Studying the subject students will get -
  1. Ability to select and use metrological instruments.
  2. Ability to apply acceptance sampling techniques and prepare tolerance chart.
