Applications of MQC :
  1. Performing the work of establishment of units and realization of the standards to the users in the society and the development of new methods in measurement.
  2. To establish importance of measurement in industry.
  3. To ensure the appropriateness of measurement instruments including the maintenance, quality control and proper calibration of the instruments.
  4. To maintain the standard quality of a product by inspecting and correcting the features of product.
